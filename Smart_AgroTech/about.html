About Smart AgroTech

Smart AgroTech is a cutting-edge project dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture through technology. Our platform integrates IoT sensors and machine learning to provide real-time soil analysis, crop recommendations, and sustainable farming practices.

We aim to empower farmers with precise, data-driven insights to maximize crop yield, reduce resource waste, and improve the overall quality of farming.

This project is designed and developed as part of a final year BE Computer Engineering initiative, leveraging Raspberry Pi sensor data, Firebase cloud services, and a user-friendly web interface.
